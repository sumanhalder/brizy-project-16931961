About Us

Welcome to Checkers & Trackers Consultancy Services Pvt Ltd! We are a leading provider of loss management services for the insurance industry in India. Our company has a strong vision to bridge the existing gaps in the industry by offering expert experience and domain knowledge, ultimately creating leaders for the long-term sustenance of the insurance market.


At Checkers & Trackers, we understand the challenges faced by insurance companies in managing losses, mitigating risks, and combating fraudulent activities. With years of experience and a deep understanding of the insurance landscape, we have built a reputation for delivering innovative solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients.


Our team of highly skilled professionals brings together a diverse range of expertise in loss management, fraud detection, risk assessment, and compliance. We are dedicated to providing our clients with the highest level of service, offering tailored strategies and solutions that drive positive outcomes.


We specialize in analyzing, training, auditing, and investigating fraudulent health insurance claims. Our advanced techniques and technologies enable us to uncover patterns, detect suspicious activities, and prevent fraudulent claims, helping insurance companies protect their financial resources and maintain the integrity of their operations.


At Checkers & Trackers, we go beyond traditional approaches. We embrace futuristic solutions that address the evolving challenges of the insurance industry. Our aim is not only to bridge the existing gaps but also to empower our clients to become leaders in the market. We believe in long-term sustenance and growth, and we work closely with our clients to develop strategies that drive profitability, minimize losses, and enhance overall business performance.


We are proud of our track record of success and the positive impact we have made on the insurance industry. Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and professionalism sets us apart. We strive to build strong and lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs and delivering personalized solutions that exceed expectations.
